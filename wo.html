Step 1: Welcome & Orientation

Your journey begins here! Orientation is designed to help you transition smoothly into university life. It's a great opportunity to get acquainted with the campus, your new classmates, and the support systems available to you.

Orientation Sessions

Attend mandatory sessions to learn about university policies, academic expectations, and campus resources. These sessions are crucial for a successful start.

Collect Your ID Card

Your student ID is your key to campus. It grants you access to the library, labs, and other facilities. Be sure to pick it up at the designated location.

Connect with Your Peers

Orientation isn't just about administrative tasks; it's about building your community. Strike up conversations with your new batchmates, participate in group activities, and start creating lasting friendships.
